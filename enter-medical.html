NEET – National Eligibility cum Entrance Exam for Medicine

NEET Details Overview

Conducted by:

National Testing Agency (NTA)

Eligibility:

Gen: 50% in PCB

OBC/SC/ST: 40%

PWD: 45%

Seats:

109,170 MBBS seats

41,388 in Govt Colleges

Application Fee:

Gen: ₹1,700

OBC: ₹1,600

Reserved: ₹1,000

Exam Month:

May (Once a year)

Success Rate:

~56% (23 lakh applicants)

INI-CET – Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test

INI-CET – National-Level PG Medical Entrance

Conducted by:

AIIMS

Eligibility:

MBBS + 12-month internship

55% Gen/OBC/EWS, 50% SC/ST

Seats:

~1,235 (AIIMS, JIPMER, PGIMER, etc.)

Application Fee:

Gen/OBC/Foreign/OCI: ₹4,000

SC/ST/EWS: ₹3,200

Exam Month:

May (Once a year)

Appearing Candidates:

~1 lakh annually

PGIMER – Post Graduate Institute of Medical Education and Research

PGIMER – Entrance for Medical & Paramedical Courses

Conducted by:

PGIMER Chandigarh (INI-CET/INI-SS by AIIMS)

Eligibility:

MD/MS: MBBS + 1-yr Internship

M.Sc Nursing: B.Sc Nursing + 2 yrs Exp.

B.Sc Nursing: 10+2 PCB + English (50%)

Seats:

Total ~610 Seats (MDS: 18, MHA: 5, B.Sc Paramedical: 257, Nursing: 93, M.Sc: 25)

Application Fee:

Gen/OBC: ₹1500

SC/ST: ₹1200

Exam Months:

December & June (Twice a year)

Appearing Candidates:

~50,000 to 60,000 annually

AIAPGET – All India AYUSH Post Graduate Entrance Test

AIAPGET – PG Entrance for Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha & Homeopathy

Conducted by:

National Testing Agency (NTA)

Eligibility:

Degree in BAMS/BUMS/BSMS/BHMS + 1-year Internship

Valid registration with council/board

Seats:

~2,274 Seats (PG Diploma/MD/MS)

Counselling by AACCC

Application Fee:

Gen/OBC: ₹2,700

Gen-EWS: ₹2,450

SC/ST/PwD: ₹1,800

Exam Month:

July (Once a year)

Appearing Candidates:

~37,980 out of 40,123 Registered

AIIMS PhD Entrance – AIIMS PhD Program Entrance Test

AIIMS PhD Entrance

Conducted by:

AIIMS (All India Institute of Medical Sciences)

Eligibility:

Master’s Degree with minimum 60% in relevant field

Seats:

52 PhD Seats (AIIMS New Delhi)

Application Fee:

General/OBC: ₹2,000

SC/ST/EWS: ₹1,600 + transaction charges

Exam Months:

January & July (Twice a year)

Appearing Candidates:

~10,000 to 15,000 annually

ICMR JRF – Indian Council of Medical Research – Junior Research Fellowship

ICMR JRF – Junior Research Fellowship

Conducted by:

Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR)

Eligibility:

M.Sc./M.A. in relevant science

55% (Gen/OBC/EWS), 50% (SC/ST/PH/VH)

Seats:

150 JRFs + 100 Project Positions

Application Fee:

General/OBC/EWS: ₹1,500

SC/ST: ₹1,200

Exam Month:

July (Once a year)

Appearing Candidates:

290 Life Sciences, 9 Social Sciences (recent)
